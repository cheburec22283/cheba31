Game Info

Mobile Legends — a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton. Expect dynamic gameplay, team battles, and a variety of heroes to choose from.

Heroes

  • 1. Gossen
  • Gossen: A high-damage assassin with swift movements and powerful abilities.
  • 2. Alucard
  • Alucard: A fighter with lifesteal capabilities and strong physical attacks.
  • 3. Benedetta
  • Benedetta: An agile assassin with crowd control and mobility skills.
  • 4. Lancelot
  • Lancelot: A mobile assassin with burst damage and evasive abilities.
  • 5. Harith
  • Harith: A mage-fighter hybrid with high mobility and area of effect damage.
  • 5. Granger
  • Granger Granger is a marksman jungler with a good start and a good late game.

Character class

  • Adk a class of long-range attacking heroes who have a long range and high physical and magical damage. Heroes of this class have a small supply of health and protection.
  • Kerri a class of heroes who are capable of inflicting high physical and magical damage, as well as having good mobility. Heroes of this class have an average reserve of health and defense.
  • Fighter balanced class. Heroes of this class have average health and defense, as well as high damage.
  • Support is a class of heroes that can heal or strengthen their allies. This class, like magicians, has the ability to stun or hinder their enemies. Heroes of this class have low health and defense.
  • Tank a class of heroes required to take the brunt of the enemy’s attack and open the way for their allies of all other classes. Heroes of this class have a large supply of health and protection, as well as average damage.

    Game Modes

    Mobile Legends: Bang Bang consists of several game modes:

    • Classic: Standard 5v5 matches on the main map.
    • Ranked Match: Competitive mode with skill-based matchmaking.
    • Brawl Mode: Quick casual matches with random heroes.
    • Survival: Battle Royale mode with resource management and elimination.

    Esports

    Mobile Legends: Bang Bang has a thriving esports scene where professional teams compete in tournaments around the world. The game has a large fan base and is a regular participant in major esports events.

    Tips & Tricks

    Share and learn gameplay strategies to improve your skills in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Here are a few tips:

    • Focus on objectives like turrets and jungle monsters.
    • Communicate with your team using the in-game chat and pings.
    • Upgrade your equipment to enhance your hero's strengths.
